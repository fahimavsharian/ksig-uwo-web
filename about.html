The Xi-Nu Chapter

Our Chapter here at Western (Xi-Nu) received its Charter on February 10th, 2018, after almost two academic years. We are one of the largest, most active, and dominant organizations on campus and also a leading Chapter for our Fraternity. From the social scene, to our academics, to our community service, to campus leadership, our chapter has placed itself in the position to become one of the strongest organizations on campus.

We are devoted to finding men who will enhance and improve our brotherhood; we are also an organization that aims to build better men. If you are committed to participating in activities for this benefit, we are looking for you!

Xi-Nu has a roll of over 100 brothers; as one of the youngest fraternities at Western University, we have recruited fall and winter classes every year since 2016 allowing us to grow our brotherhood consistently year after year. Our members are leaders and hold membership in many campus organizations as well as organizations across the country.

Our chapter prides itself on being the largest and strongest fraternity in London. As such, joining Kappa Sigma means gaining one of the strongest support groups a University freshman can ask for. Advice, guidance, and opportunities for personal growth are all at your very fingertips.
